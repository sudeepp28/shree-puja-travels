Arunachalam Temple (Arunachaleswarar Temple), Tamil Nadu

The Arunachalam Temple, also known as Arunachaleswarar Temple , is one of the most sacred temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. Located in Tiruvannamalai, Tamil Nadu, this temple is one of the Pancha Bhoota Sthalams, representing the element Fire (Agni).


This spiritual destination draws lakhs of devotees every year, especially during Karthigai Deepam, when a giant flame is lit atop the holy Arunachala Hill.
